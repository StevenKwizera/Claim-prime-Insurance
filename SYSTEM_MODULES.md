# Prime Insurance — Digital Claims Verification & Decision System

**Not a financial system.** No payments, billing, premiums, or payouts are handled in this platform.

Users submit claims → the system and AI verify documents and flag risk → **officers make the final decision** → outcomes are recorded as approved or rejected.

---

## Core modules (10)

1. **User Authentication & Access Control** — register, login, roles, admin user management, security settings  
2. **Digital Claim Submission** — guided intake, drafts, confirmation reference  
3. **Evidence Management** — upload, gallery, secure preview  
4. **AI Document Verification** — pending queue, AI results, manual override  
5. **AI Fraud Detection** — risk scores, flagged claims, investigator workspace  
6. **Claim Evaluation & Decision** — approve, reject, request info, internal notes  
7. **Claim Tracking** — status timeline, claimant tracking  
8. **Communication & Notification Center** — in-app notifications and templates  
9. **Reporting & Compliance** — reports, compliance, audit trail  
10. **Data Analytics & Insights** — dashboards and trends for oversight roles  

---

## User activities

### Claimant
- Register / login  
- Submit claim  
- Upload evidence  
- Track claim status  
- Respond to requests  
- Receive approval or rejection notifications  

### Officer
- Review claims  
- Check AI results  
- Verify evidence  
- Approve or reject claims  
- Request more information  

### Investigator
- Review flagged claims  
- Investigate fraud alerts  
- Report findings  

### Admin
- Manage users  
- Configure system rules (security policies)  
- Monitor login activity / audit logs  
- View reports and analytics  

### AI (support only)
- Analyze documents  
- Detect fraud risk  
- Generate risk scores  
- Support officers — **not** the final decision maker
